Featured Fish Species

Lapu-Lapu (Grouper)

Epinephelus fuscoguttatus

Found in coral reefs across the Philippines, this fish is known for its delicious meat and is a staple in Filipino cuisine.

Location: All major islands

Bangus (Milkfish)

Chanos chanos

The national fish of the Philippines, bangus is widely farmed and is known for its silver scales and tender meat.

Location: Pangasinan, Bulacan, Batangas

Maya-Maya (Red Snapper)

Lutjanus argentimaculatus

A premium fish with reddish skin and white flesh, commonly found in deeper waters around the archipelago.

Location: Visayas, Mindanao

Tambakol (Tuna)

Thunnus albacares

A commercially important fish species, the Philippines is one of the world's major tuna producers.

Location: General Santos, Mindanao

Brown Tang

Acanthurus nigrofuscus

A commercially important fish species, the Philippines is one of the world's major tuna producers.

Location: General Santos, Mindanao

Submission Guidelines

  • Please provide clear, high-quality photos that show the fish's distinctive features.
  • Be as specific as possible about the location where you discovered the fish.
  • If you know the scientific name, please include it, but it's not required.
  • Include information about the fish's habitat, behavior, and any other notable characteristics.
  • Our team will review your submission and may contact you for additional information.
  • Verified discoveries will be added to our database with credit to you as the contributor.

About TalaIsda

Learn about our mission to document and preserve Philippine fish biodiversity

Our Story

TalaIsda (from the Filipino words "tala" meaning list or catalog and "isda" meaning fish) was established in 2025 by a group of students from National University- Manila, IT students who shared a common passion for Philippine marine biodiversity.

The Philippines, being at the heart of the Coral Triangle, is home to one of the richest marine ecosystems in the world. Despite this, there was a lack of accessible, comprehensive information about the country's fish species, especially those endemic to specific regions.

TalaIsda was born out of the need to create a centralized, user-friendly platform that documents and celebrates the diverse fish species found in Philippine waters. Our goal is not only to serve as an educational resource but also to promote conservation awareness and encourage community participation in preserving our aquatic treasures.

Our Mission

To document, catalog, and promote awareness of the diverse fish species in the Philippines through an accessible and comprehensive digital platform, fostering a deeper appreciation for marine biodiversity and supporting conservation efforts.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every fish species is documented, appreciated, and protected, with communities actively engaged in sustainable practices that ensure the preservation of aquatic ecosystems for future generations.

What We Do

TalaIsda serves as a comprehensive directory of fish species found in the Philippines, organized by geographical location. Our platform offers:

  • Detailed information about various fish species, including their local names, scientific classification, and habitat.
  • Regional categorization to help users discover species specific to different parts of the Philippines
  • A community-driven approach that allows users to contribute their own discoveries
